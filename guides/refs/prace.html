Desc. :

Aarakocran are bipedal birdmen, with hands incorporated into their wings.
Aarakocran are a little taller than humans, but much lighter due to their
bone structure. They are adept flyers with superior vision. Due to their
lack of arms, Aarakocrans have great difficulty in dressing themselves.
